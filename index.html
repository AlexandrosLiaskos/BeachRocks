Welcome to Beach Rocks

Welcome to Beach Rocks, an interactive map for exploring the global distribution of beachrocks - coastal sedimentary formations cemented in the intertidal zone.

Dataset: 384 Beachrock Sites | Coverage: 50 Countries across 11 Oceans & Seas

This interactive map is built upon the global beachrock database published by Evelpidou, N. et al. (2025) on Zenodo.

How to cite:

Evelpidou, N., Saitis, G., Karkani, A., & Spyrou, E. (2025). Global database of beachrock distribution (v.1.0) [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.16408107
